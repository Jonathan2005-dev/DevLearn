💡 Headings and Text

HTML provides six levels of headings (h1-h6) for organizing content. <h1> is the most important, typically used for main titles, while <h6> is the least important.

<p> tags define paragraphs, <em> for emphasis (italic), <strong> for strong emphasis (bold), and other formatting tags like <mark>, <del>, and <ins>.
